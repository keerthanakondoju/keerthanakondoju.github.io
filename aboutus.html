Cafe Belle Vie is synonymous with great food, good ambiance and fun times. There is live karaoke and music on weekends by local musicians. Friday evenings are covered with laughter, organized by stand-up comic artists.... This said it's a great place to hangout and simply catch up friends and family. It's where simple life overtakes boredom and mundane monotony - with just a little extra - your favourite pastas, mojitos, sandwiches and cappuccinos thrown in.
